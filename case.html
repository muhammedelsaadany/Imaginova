The story of ½ M’s journey with Imaginova is that it took plenty of planning, implementation, and effort. Brand ½ M, an Egyptian brand that started in 2020, initially opened several branches but failed due to many challenges. The owner then traveled to Dubai to learn how to make high-quality doughnuts and chocolates. Upon returning to Egypt, he opened his branch in Tanta and embarked on a mission to create the most delicious doughnuts you could ever taste.

Our Role

Firstly, we scanned the market and identified local competitors. Then, we analyzed the common prices.
After that, we figured out why some products weren't selling well and identified market opportunities.
We worked on fixing these issues to improve sales.

Brand Designing Process

Explored innovative methods to integrate donuts and chocolates into the brand's visual identity. Crafted stylish and attention-grabbing packaging designs that mirrored the brand's essence and high-end standards. Utilized refined color palettes and graphical elements to evoke a sense of allure and excitement.

Challenge: Standing out in a crowded market.
Solution: Leveraged creative elegance to create a distinctive brand identity that captured attention and differentiated 1/2 M from competitors.

Results and Impact:

  • Enhanced brand visibility and awareness within the local market.
  • Elevated customer interaction and loyalty, evident in heightened repeat purchases and favorable testimonials.
  • Attained significant growth in sales and revenue, surpassing initial projections

Our primary focus remains on meeting client expectations and exercising sound judgment :)
