Outsourcing

Web Development & Programming:

Outsourcing web development services involves hiring external developers or specialized firms to build, update, or maintain websites, web applications, or e-commerce platforms for agency clients.

User Experience (UX) Design:

Agencies may outsource UX design services to professionals who specialize in creating intuitive and user friendly digital experiences. This involves conducting user research, creating wireframes, and designing user interfaces for websites or  applications.

Financial & Legal Services:

Outsourcing financial functions like bookkeeping, tax preparation, or auditing, as well as legal services like contract drafting or intellectual property management, are also common practices.
